About

This map diplays all current BAPL dealers and is filterable by Territory Manager. Pins are color coded by the Dealer's Main Line brand. Johne Deere is green, CASE is red, AGCO is yellow, New Holland is blue, and all other brands are represented by purple. It is possible to overlay each territory's boundarys on the map to get a sense of the dealer distribution within the territory. It is possible to search for a specific geographic location (seaching by name of Dealer won't work) and selecting a desired radius to show the number of dealers within that radius. It is also possible to overlay the John Deere agricultural dealerships that have been identified as potential prospects.
